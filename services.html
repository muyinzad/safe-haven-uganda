Our Services

Comprehensive support designed to meet the unique needs of transgender women in Uganda

Healthcare and Support Services

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right. We provide education, referrals, and navigation support to help transgender women access inclusive, affirming healthcare services across Uganda.

Our team works with trusted healthcare providers and organizations to ensure you receive the care you deserve in a safe, confidential environment.

HIV and STI Services

We provide comprehensive HIV and sexually transmitted infection support, connecting you with testing services, treatment education, and prevention resources.

  • Free HIV testing guidance
  • PrEP education and referrals
  • PEP emergency access
  • ART adherence support
  • Viral load monitoring info
  • STI testing and treatment
  • Condom and lubricant access
  • Prevention counseling

Safety and Relocation Support

Many transgender women face threats, eviction, family rejection, and violence. We offer comprehensive safety planning and relocation assistance.

  • Emergency safety planning
  • Temporary safe housing
  • Relocation within Uganda
  • International protection guidance
  • Documentation support
  • Case management
  • Crisis response team
  • Legal referrals

Mental Health and Psychosocial Support

Stigma and discrimination can take a heavy toll. We provide mental health support to help you navigate challenges and build resilience.

  • Peer support groups
  • Trauma-informed counseling
  • Crisis intervention
  • Depression and anxiety support
  • Suicide prevention resources
  • Community workshops
  • One-on-one sessions
  • Family mediation

Know Your Rights

Understanding your rights is essential. We provide legal education and connect you with trusted human rights organizations.

  • Rights education
  • Legal guidance
  • Human rights referrals
  • Documentation assistance
  • Emergency services access
  • Police assistance
  • Confidential support
  • Advocacy support

Community and Empowerment

We believe in strength through unity. Our community programs help transgender women connect, learn, and grow together.

  • Skills training
  • Economic empowerment
  • Small business support
  • Leadership development
  • Community forums
  • Safe networking
  • Mentorship programs
  • Cultural events
