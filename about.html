About Safe Haven Uganda

Empowering transgender women through advocacy, healthcare, and community support

Who We Are

A Safe Space for Every Trans Woman

Safe Haven Uganda was founded with a simple belief: every transgender woman deserves safety, healthcare, respect, and opportunity — no matter who she is or where she lives.

We are a dedicated platform supporting transgender women across Uganda. Our existence is rooted in the fundamental truth that human rights are universal and inalienable. Despite the challenges posed by discriminatory laws and societal stigma, we continue to fight for the dignity and rights of our community.

Our team consists of passionate advocates, healthcare coordinators, counselors, and community leaders who work tirelessly to ensure that no trans woman has to face life's challenges alone.

💜

"Trans Women Are Leaders, Entrepreneurs, Caregivers, & Changemakers"

🎯

Our Mission

To empower transgender women in Uganda through comprehensive human rights advocacy, access to sexual and reproductive healthcare, HIV & STI prevention and treatment support, anti-stigma education, relocation and safety assistance, and mental health and psychosocial support.

🔮

Our Vision

A Uganda where every transgender woman lives with dignity, free from discrimination and violence, with equal access to healthcare, employment, and opportunity. A society that recognizes the inherent worth and contributions of all transgender people.

Our Story

Born from Need, Built on Resilience

Safe Haven Uganda was established in response to the growing needs of transgender women facing heightened discrimination, violence, and exclusion. As laws changed and societal challenges increased, our founders recognized the urgent need for a dedicated support system.

What started as a small support group has grown into a comprehensive organization offering advocacy, healthcare navigation, emergency assistance, and community empowerment programs.

Today, we continue to expand our reach and services, always keeping the safety and dignity of transgender women at the center of everything we do.
