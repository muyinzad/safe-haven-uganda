Our Programs

Empowering transgender women through education, skills training, and community building

What We Offer

Empowerment Programs

Our programs are designed to build skills, create opportunities, and strengthen community bonds.

Education and Awareness

Fighting stigma through education. We work to create a more informed and accepting society.

  • Community workshops
  • Healthcare provider training
  • School awareness programs
  • Media campaigns
  • Resource materials

Economic Empowerment

Building financial independence through skills training and entrepreneurship support.

  • Vocational training
  • Business development
  • Job placement support
  • Microfinance access
  • Mentorship programs

Peer Support Network

Building connections and providing mutual support through community networks.

  • Support groups
  • Community events
  • Mentorship matching
  • Safe spaces
  • Online community

Advocacy and Rights

Amplifying voices and advocating for policy change and human rights.

  • Rights education
  • Policy advocacy
  • Legal support
  • Community mobilization
  • Coalition building

Health and Wellness

Promoting physical and mental health through education and support services.

  • Health workshops
  • Exercise groups
  • Nutrition guidance
  • Mental health support
  • Healthcare navigation

Leadership Development

Nurturing the next generation of trans leaders and community advocates.

  • Leadership training
  • Public speaking
  • Advocacy skills
  • Networking events
  • Scholarship programs
Making a Difference

Our Impact

"Changing lives, one person at a time"

Through our programs, we've seen remarkable transformations. From young trans women gaining employment to survivors finding safety, our community continues to thrive despite challenges.

  • 200+ trained in vocational skills
  • 50+ started small businesses
  • 500+ in peer support groups
  • 50+ community workshops
